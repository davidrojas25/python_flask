Warriors come back super charged from National Championship Experience!

What an amazing experience to match the amazing accomplishment by our warriors this last season! Our warriors traveled to Madison WI to race against the very best runners in the country!braving the cold and travel and placing amounts the very best in those races! Besides the incredible racing experience our warriors had a chance to participate in opening ceremonies, pre-race dinner, team meetings, visit the WI state capitol, tour of Chicago, and even a trip to the tallest buildings! Not to mention ice skating, world famous deep dish pizza, and trolley ride! Fun and excitement was the name of the game on this trip! To say our kids enjoyed themselves is an understatement. On top of that it was exciting to see the energy these kids are bringing back to the rest of the team and to our program overall! Eager to start the next season and accomplish even more! As huge as this event was for us we hope to take it a step further in 2020 and with the energy of both kids and parents I have no doubt we will grow and accomplish more very soon! Stay tuned on what your Warriors are up to next! Enjoy the slide show!
